About us

The Krusty Krab! Come spend your money here!

The Krusty Krab is a fast food restaurant located in Bikini Bottom,
founded and owned by Eugene H. Krabs.
It is also the most popular and best known restaurant in Bikini Bottom.

Famous for its Krabby Patty burgers,
it is a rival to Plankton's highly unpopular
across-the-street restaurant called the Chum Bucket.
Its employees are SpongeBob SquarePants and Squidward
Tentacles, respectively the fry cook and cashier.
